Interior Design Firm

Jaguar Kraafts Interior Design Studio has been creating elegant and practical interior spaces for almost two decades (20 years ). As a luxury home designer firm based in the cosmopolitan hub like Pune and cultural hub like, we work with a varied range of clients in India. Our portfolio includes a variety of industry verticals including Residential, Hospitality, Corporate & commercial spaces. Our designs are a reflection of our passion of good work and that our commitment to meet and exceed our client’s expectations is reflected in our work. Our Technical expertise is portrayed in the work executed by us.

At Jaguar Kraafts, we turn our unique experiences, practical approach, and creative flair into great designs. Our creative team of luxury and budget based home designers is what sets our brand with unmatched customer service. Our design philosophy is to knit together the creative and practical elements to create a well designed and functional end product.

We pour our heart to design every little detail, so be it getting the furnitures customized or selecting lamps- everything has a touch of creativity. Be it an apartment design or luxury villa interiors; a lot of attention and creativity goes in along with a personal touch of care resulting in a well-coordinated and balanced interior.

We deliver a premium turn-key luxury home designing service. We come up with conceptual designs after thoroughly understanding the client’s wish list. Concepts are then converted to the 3d visualisation which is further converted to working drawings.

Thus converting the visualisation into reality and thereby turning the clients requirement into existing and weaving all the thoughts into a creation. Our designs are customized to suit the clients requirement and style desired and thereby transforming dreams into reality.

Our Design Approach

As a home designer firm, our responsibility is to understand the client needs and design spaces and products with utmost standards and superior quality. We look forward to improve the overall quality and standard of life for the clients by delivering not just beautiful and functional designs but smart and user friendly spaces which is achieved by understanding the requirement and the style preferences.

The transform of the design inputs to visualisation and their further transformation into drawings result into a harmonious space.

We also design Hospitality & commercial spaces like Showrooms Restaurants and offices. Be it a residential or a commercial project, Jaguar Kraafts offers the hands-on attention to all the spaces.

Our Services

Residential

We give you the luxury you can live in. Our luxury home designs combine style and substance to give you the perfect forever home. Every element, colour, texture, and item is selected with care to transform a blank space into a warm, tasteful luxury residence filled with meaning. With our residential interior design services, you can relax and let the experts take care of everything. Our team gives you a seamless luxury home designer service – from concept design and 3D rendering to furniture layout to final decor and styling. We put in our all at every step of the design process done in-house; giving you a complete and customizable interior design solution for your home.

Commercial

The interiors of your business have an effect on your employees, customers, and clients. So, why not get the very best? Our commercial spaces are thoughtfully planned to inspire creativity, boost productivity, drive sales and to develop and communicate the unique image of your brand and business. Our team of commercial luxury interior designers in Mumbai are experts at creating beautiful interiors for your enterprise. Whether you need a dynamic office interior or a welcoming retail space – we have your back.

Corporate

Corporate services collectively contribute to the efficient functioning and growth of a business by addressing various operational, administrative, and strategic needs.

Hospitality

Effective hospitality services aim to create a positive and welcoming atmosphere, fostering customer loyalty and satisfaction. The success of businesses in the hospitality industry often depends on their ability to consistently deliver high-quality services and exceed guest expectations.
