Benefits of 3D Rendering in Interior Design

By Maria Dinshaw | Aug 2021

A picture is worth a thousand words. This is especially true for 3D rendered pictures.

3D Rendering is the process of creating a three-dimensional scale drawing of a proposed plan. Made using computer graphics, these drawings are photo-realistic and impressive visuals. Through 3D rendering, interior designers show the varied components of space in a compelling way. This is a critical tool for creating designs that are understandable and informative.

Nitido Design is a leading luxury interior designer in Mumbai. We are always looking for novel ways to improve our design methods and client experience. 3D rendering is one of our preferred resources, especially for modern residential interior design. In this blog, we are letting you in on the basics and benefits of 3D rendering in luxury interior design.

Benefits of 3D Rendering for the Interior Designer

The field of interior design is constantly evolving. One the creative end, there are always innovative ways that interior designers create and beautify built spaces. On the technical side, the field is updating with new and advanced technologies. As luxury interior designers, we stay in touch with these developments to design to the best of our abilities. We look for ample opportunities to learn and play with new applications. 3D rendering is one such technology and it provides an exciting way to elevate our work.

Creating Better and Improved Designs

The primary responsibility as interior designers is to create a functional design. 3D rendering proves to be a great addition to your design arsenal to achieve that. The ease, speed, accuracy, and finesse with which you can visualize your designs is unparalleled. 3D rendering allows you to make designs in a more effective manner than 2D sketches or plans. Most interior design rendering softwares come with a range of exciting features. Certain features create hyper realistic renderings that evoke lively imagery. Others incorporate legal guidelines into your package so that your design is compliant. These features also help create an aesthetic and appealing design. Through a feature enriched software, you can better convey your vision for a space. The visual competence of 3D rendering is superior to traditional methods of presentation.

Saving Time and Money

Creating a 3D version of your proposed plan allows you to see your design with greater clarity. From the layout to furniture to appliances, all elements are on one platform. Different layers can be pulled apart and put together so you can visualize the space from inside out. This makes it easier to identify mistakes and make consequent amendments. This goes a long way in saving money and ensuring you stay within budget. Working in luxury interior design, we want to ensure that our designs are worthy of the time and investment of our clients. 

You can also try out different designs for the same space without having to build a single thing. Be it trying out different colours on the wall or different upholsteries for the sofas. Be it different textures on the furniture or finishes on wooden panels. Comparing alternatives can be done in a flash. This eliminates a lot of time-consuming and administrative work.  You no longer need to coordinate with vendors, organize swatches and samples. This gives you and your team more time to focus on your design work.  

Communicating with ease and clarity

Being able to communicate your designs in a more efficient way is very useful. It gives you an undeniable competitive edge with clients. It helps bridge the gap between what you see and what they understand. The flexibility of 3D rendering also allows you to create designs adapted to the clients’ preference. For instance, with business owners who understand the language of facts and figures. You can create a more technical 3D rendering that highlights the layout and structure of the design. With a redecorating project, you want a design that is attractive and engaging. With 3D rendering, you can compare and contrast the existing space and the potential changes in a dynamic manner. In our experience, 3D rendering leads to an increase in design approvals. This leads to a smoother worker relationship between the designer and the client.

Nitido Design is a top residential interior design firm in the cosmopolitan city of Mumbai. We work with clients from across the country and the world. 3D rendering helps us transcend language and communication barriers. A high quality presentation keeps the focus on the design. With good and sound design, we get to speak to the mind and heart of our diverse clientele.

 Working as a cohesive team

Designing interiors requires a team of designers, manufacturers, labourers, builders, and artists. All the best projects come to fruition  with the combined talents of a team. Coordinating and communicating with different members is a crucial part of making a design a reality. Through 3D rendering it becomes easier to work as a cohesive team. You can convey your creative ideas and visions to the team before production even starts. This ensures greater clarity which leaves less room for misconceptions and errors. Interior design is a field that embodies the idea that something can be greater than the sum of its parts. Smaller components come together to create a holistic and beautiful space. Similarly, an interior design team can create true synergy and harmony through open communication and feedback.  3D rendering is an essential tool for that.

Creating a dynamic portfolio

Cultivating a habit of making 3D renderings of your work is useful for a dynamic portfolio. This is important for interior design firms as well as individual designers. A library of 3D rendering makes it easy to showcase and navigate the different projects you have worked. You can also show the many stages and iterations of a specific project. This gives potential clients and collaborators a look into your unique design process. A competent and dynamic portfolio is a must-have resource. It opens you and your firm up to future collaborations and potential clients. 

Benefits of 3D Rendering to Clients

3D rendering is as effective a tool for designers as it is for clients. Since it is a resource that is easy to use and understand; it makes design a more approachable subject. One does not need extensive technical knowledge to grasp the basic visualization of the space. It is useful to understand the benefits of 3D rendering for non-designers. Interior design firms can then use the tool strategically to bring clients on board and keep them involved. This helps firms improve and deepen their client relationships.

Increasing client participation

3D rendering essentially adds more control in the hands of the client. It allows them to alter and transform designs to their liking. This increases their participation in their own project. Since they are the ones that would be approving the design and using the space, their contributions can prove to be vital. With a user-friendly rendering software, clients can change, replace, and shift elements with ease.

This gives the interior designer a better sense of what the client is expecting. The conventional method was for designers to create proposals to send to the client. They would go back and forth until a plan gets finalized. With 3D rending, client participation increases. This turns the initial stages of a project into an active and fruitful discussion.

Improving understanding of projects

Showing distinctive lights and shadows in a luxury interior space. Depicting a rippling effect of wind on a balcony garden. Adding vibrant colours and textures to a commercial estate. Designers can make their visions come alive on screen with 3D rendering. They can illustrate real life depictions of different elements of their design. As a client, you can ask your interior designer to adapt the 3D rendering to your needs. You can ask for technical clarity in an easy to understand manner. You can navigate the space virtually and get a better feel of the space. 3D rendering helps you get a see and understand the project better.

Enhancing Client Satisfaction

For the overall satisfaction of the client, it is vital to give them the best look into their project. With 3D renderings, interior designers can develop more inviting and memorable presentations. Virtual reality views, animated videos in 3D spaces, 360 degree photos, and videos and 3D walkthroughs are just skimming the list. There are more and more creative ways to connect with your audience. It is an easy decision to choose to hone your 3D rendering skills.

3D rendering: Make your designs come alive

3D rendering can take your design to a whole other level. As a designer, it can help you create more impressive designs. It supports you in sharing your aesthetic vision with your clients.

As a client, it helps you visualize the proposed plan. So, you can ensure that it fits your vision and company ethos. 3D rendering is the essential bridge between the interior designer and the client.

Elevate your design process. Improve your client relationship. Make your designs come alive.
