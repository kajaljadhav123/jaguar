What is the renovation process for a luxury house?

By Maria Dinshaw | Aug 2021

Deciding to renovate your home can be quite a task. Whether you are tweaking a room or redesigning your whole house – renovation requires time and effort. As a luxury home designer, Nitido Design has worked on a variety of renovation projects. We have come up with innovative interior design ideas for remodeling small houses. We have planned extensive renovations for luxury homes. Renovations are in our experience and expertise. If you are asking yourself if you should renovate, this is the decision-making guide for you! There is also a bonus list of questions to ask your interior design firm before working with them!

What about your house is not working for you?

Do a little introspection before embarking on the renovation journey. Narrow down on what you would like to change in your home. Is the flow and structure of the rooms not working for you? Is it the storage and space falling short? Do you want to freshen up the style of your house? Once you have found the issue you would like to work on, ask if renovation can solve that problem? If yes, then read on! 

It is quite natural for you and your family to outgrow your house. Your needs change with time. With a renovation, your home can be tailored to your new requirements. With the right plan, you can customize your space to be more functional and aesthetic.

How much do you want to invest?

You have identified the issue that a potential remodel can fix. How much do you want to invest in this renovation? Depending on the scale of changes needed, you will have a list of expenses. Fees for professional interior design services and cost of materials are some of the broad categories. You will also have to budget in a little extra for unexpected hidden costs. While a renovation presents a lot of upfront costs, there is an advantage of investing in where you live. Updating your home increases the value of your home. This is important if you are considering selling it in the future.

What design firm do I want to work with?

The next step in your decision-making process is choosing a design firm. Picking the right luxury interior designer for your house will lead to a smoother and more enjoyable process. It is crucial that your luxury home designer resonates with your priorities and personality.

 Check out their earlier work

The first step is to check out the previous work of the luxury home designers that interest you. Most luxury design firms have a library of their work available on their website. Dig into them to familiarize yourself with their design style and methods. Have they worked on many renovation projects? Is their style more minimalist or classic?

How is their general aesthetic? If the situation permits, you can also visit their model homes or previous projects. There is nothing like walking through a space to get a feel of it.

Meet with the interior design firm

Meeting with potential interior design firms is the best way to get to know them. Schedule a meeting with the luxury interior designers of your interest. Do your research prior and ask all the questions you have. This will give you an idea of their work and communication style. Talk to them about your ideas for the renovation and your personal style. Any good design firm will make a competent design proposal. But the right design firm for you will be able to create a design that fits your creative vision.

Bonus list of questions to ask interior design firms

Don’t know what to ask while interviewing your design firm. Here is our list of questions to ask luxury home designers before working with them.

What interior design services do you offer?

How do you prioritize the client’s budget throughout the project?

How much client collaboration do you welcome?

Where do you get your inspiration? What elements define your style?

How do you decide which projects are a good fit for your firm?

Who will be working on my project? What is your communication style?

In case of unanticipated issues, how do you deal with them?

How can Nitido Design help you with your renovation process?

Nitido Design understands that a renovation has different challenges than designing an untouched space. We are not starting on a blank canvas, but on a lived in home. We take great care to work with the existing space and not against it. We design renovations and restorations that are technically sound and aesthetically pleasing. We strive to complete the project within our deadlines, so you can have a new and upgraded home as soon as possible. 

Do you want to know more about how Nitido Design can help you with your renovation process? Reach out to us with your questions! Our dedicated team of renovators will be more happy to answer!
