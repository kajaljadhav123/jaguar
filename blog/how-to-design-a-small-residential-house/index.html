Easy Step-by-Step Guide to Designing a Small House in 2021

By Maria Dinshaw | Aug 2021

Small houses are easy to maintain, comfortable to live in, and can fit all your needs – without breaking the bank. Whether you moved to a big city where a studio apartment is an apt and affordable option. Or are a young family, purchasing your first house and designing on a budget. Small houses are practical and accessible. Moreover, having a small house does not take away from the joy of designing a space.

Constraints foster creativity. At Nidito Design, we love residential interior design for small houses because it pushes us to come with innovative designs. Some of our best interior design ideas and services come from working in a limited space.

 Here is our Easy Step-by-Step Guide to Designing a Small House in 2021

Design an Effective Layout

The first step of designing any space is deciding the layout. A good design is quite literally built on a strong and effective layout. A small space benefits from an open floor plan. This design has gained considerable popularity in residential and commercial interior design. It allows you to move elements around and transform the space according to your needs. It gives you a lot of room to play with. You can create zones within the room using partitions. This is a less bulky alternative to walls and you can have fun with them. Install a woven cane divider or a partial glass partition that breaks the space and allows light in. Show off your artistic sense and create segments using a large painting instead!

The key idea is that space can serve many uses such as in multipurpose rooms. For example, you can merge the kitchen and the dining area or add a study within the guest bedroom. Pair this with lots of windows and increase the natural light coming in.  To know more design hacks, check this blog we wrote.

 Invest in Functional and Aesthetic Furniture

Investing in the right furniture can go a long way in making your space functional and aesthetic. Since every piece stands out in a small interior, it is important to choose pieces that work for you. For living and dining spaces, go with round tables that not only save space but also look more welcoming. For the bedroom, opt for a full or queen bed and watch as your bedroom doubles in size. Regain precious floor space by mounting your TV, speakers, microwaves, and other appliances.

You can also hide them by building a screen or sliding door in front of the mount. Go for versatile furniture that can serve more than one use such as nested tables or sofas with movable ottomans. For seating options, there is a range of premium foldable chairs available now. Choose high quality pieces that take up less space and look good doing it too!

 Get Smart with Storage

Nitido Design is a top residential interior design firm based in Mumbai. Many of our clients come from high-density urban areas where every square foot is treasured. So when it comes to designing storage in small residential interiors, we use every inch available. The trick to creating efficient storage is to customize it to suit your needs. With built-in storage, you can organize your house in an effective and discrete manner. Make cabinets with trays that pull out and retrieve items with ease. Optimize your cabinet with built-in closet organizers and baskets. Include a turntable in larger closets to maximize space.

Also, think vertical. Use open shelves in your study to replace clunky cabinets. Use the kitchen walls to display your pots and pans with a minimal wire rack. Keep the entrance of your home clutter free with smart storage for shoes, keys, and knick knacks.

 Pick Clever and Creative Decor

There are so many avenues for creative interior design in a small residential house. Paint your walls in a bright and neutral colour palette to open the space. Pair classic white shades with beige, light grey, sea salt blue, or cream – the possibilities are endless. Show off your colour choices with the right lighting and decor pieces that will illuminate the space. Mirrors are the go-to decor item to reflect light within a room. Take it up a notch and incorporate lacquered walls, glossy tiles, and other reflective materials into your interior design. These revive a space while giving you clever and creative options to choose from. 

 Design with Love

In a small residential space, every detail counts. Infuse your space with energy by letting your personality shine through in each piece. Design your house with love and watch it transform into your home.
