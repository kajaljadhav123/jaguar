What are the Principles of Interior Design?

Interior design is all about creating a space that reflects the needs and personality of the people who live there. While there are no hard-and-fast rules, here are a few principles that the best interior designers in Mumbai and Pune always keep in mind.

  • The first is to create a sense of balance. This can be achieved by using symmetry and proportion to create an appealing and harmonious space.

  • The second principle is to create a focal point. This is usually achieved through the use of color, light, or pattern.

  • The third principle is to create a sense of unity. This can be done by using repetition and rhythm to connect different elements of the room.

By following these principles, the best interior designers at Nitido Design will take the time to get to know you and your needs, and then use their expertise to create a space that will be perfect for you.
